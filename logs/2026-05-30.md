## 📝 English Sentence

> *"Immersive virtual reality environments have demonstrated potential in enhancing experiential learning by allowing learners to engage with simulated scenarios that would otherwise be inaccessible, dangerous, or prohibitively costly to reproduce in real-world settings."*

**Topic:** VR / Experiential Learning

---

## Step 1: Translation（日本語訳）

**健さんの訳：**
> 没入型仮想空間環境（VR環境）は、学習者が模擬されたシナリオに従事することで現実世界の設定では到達できない、危険な、または禁止された行為を経験し、それにより、経験による学習を強化する潜在能力があることが実証されている。

**フィードバック：**
- ✅ 文構造の把握：良好
- ⚠️ `prohibitively costly to reproduce` → 「禁止された行為」ではなく「費用的に現実的でない」が正確
- ⚠️ `in real-world settings` → 「現実世界の設定では」より「現実の環境では」が自然

**修正後の訳（参考）：**
> 没入型VR環境は、現実の環境では到達困難・危険・または再現に費用がかかりすぎるシナリオを学習者が体験できるようにすることで、経験的学習を強化する潜在能力があることが示されている。

- 総評：**B+**

## Step 2: English Response（英語コメント）

**健さんのコメント：**
> That's true of your view. However, these virtual reality environment affect learners to "Real", "Dangerous", kind of visual based feelings are occupied. Some researcher mentioned that it doesn't mean in short-term education program. Based on this views, we have to reschedule how to use VR education is more valuable for leaners.

**修正案：**
> This is a valid point. However, VR environments tend to evoke feelings of reality and danger primarily through visual stimulation, which may not fully replicate genuine experience. Some researchers have argued that such effects may not be meaningful in short-term educational programs. Based on these views, we need to reconsider how VR-based education can be designed to be more effective for learners.

**主な修正点：**

| 元の表現 | 修正後 | 理由 |
|----------|--------|------|
| `That's true of your view` | `This is a valid point` | "That's true of"は不自然。学術的にはこちら |
| `these virtual reality environment` | `these virtual reality environments` | 複数形 |
| `affect learners to "Real", "Dangerous"` | `evoke feelings of "reality" and "danger" in learners` | affect〜toの構造が不正確 |
| `kind of visual based feelings are occupied` | `through primarily visual stimulation` | 文構造が崩れている |
| `Some researcher mentioned` | `Some researchers have argued` | 複数形・学術的動詞 |
| `it doesn't mean in short-term` | `such effects may not be sustained in short-term programs` | 意味が曖昧、補足が必要 |
| `reschedule` | `reconsider` / `redesign` | rescheduleは「日程変更」の意味 |
| `more valuable for leaners` | `more effective for learners` | スペルミス・語彙 |

- 総評：**B**

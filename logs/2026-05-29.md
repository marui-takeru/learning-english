# Day 1 — 2026-05-29

## 📝 English Sentence

> *"Community resilience is not merely a function of physical infrastructure, but is deeply embedded in the social capital and collective efficacy of local residents, particularly their capacity to organize and respond autonomously in the aftermath of a disaster."*

**Topic:** Community Resilience / Disaster Risk Reduction

---

## Step 1: Translation（日本語訳）

**健さんの訳：**
> コミュニティ・レジリエンスは単に物理的な社会基盤としての機能だけでなく、地元住民の社会関係資本や集団的効力感の中に深く埋め込まれており、特に災害の余波において住民の組織力と自律的に応答する能力として現れる。

**フィードバック：**
- ✅ 文構造の把握：完璧
- ⚠️ `physical infrastructure` → 「物理的インフラ」がより自然（「社会基盤としての機能」は意訳過剰）
- 総評：**A**

---

## Step 2: English Response（英語コメント）

**健さんのコメント：**
> I agree with this opinion. In my experience, I found the most of people are misunderstanding of this concept. Most of people including academical researcher, often be focused on prevention from disaster, particularly disaster awareness or evacuation behaviour and so on. However, the most important concepts is not only for disaster but also maintaining the region. That's why, I think Community resilience is important, but difficult to promote.

**修正案：**
> I agree with this view. In my experience, I find that most people — including academic researchers — often focus only on disaster prevention, particularly disaster awareness or evacuation behaviour. However, the most important concept extends beyond disaster response to encompass the broader sustaining of communities. This is why I believe community resilience is critically important, yet difficult to promote.

**主な修正点：**

| 元の表現 | 修正後 | 理由 |
|----------|--------|------|
| `the most of people` | `most people` | "the most of"は不自然 |
| `often be focused on` | `often focus on` | 動詞形が不正確 |
| `academical researcher` | `academic researchers` | "academical"は古語・複数形必要 |
| `the most important concepts is` | `the most important concept is` | 単複の一致 |
| `That's why` | `This is why` | 論文・学会ではこちらが自然 |

- 総評：**B+**

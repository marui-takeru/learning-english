# Day 6 — 2026-06-19

## 📝 English Sentence

> *"Effective leadership in disaster contexts does not require leaders to project invulnerability; rather, it demands the courage to acknowledge one's own limitations and to seek support from the very community one aims to serve."*

**Topic:** Leadership / Vulnerability in Disaster Contexts

---

## Step 1: Translation（日本語訳）

**健さんの訳：**
> 災害下における効果的なリーダーシップはプロジェクトで無敵なリーダを求めるものではない、むしろ、生き延びる目的のもと自らの限界を知り、たくさんのコミュニティからのサポートに務める勇気があ流人材を要求する

**フィードバック：**
- ⚠️ `project invulnerability` → `project`はここでは動詞「（〜であると）示す・印象づける」。「プロジェクト」と名詞で誤読
- ⚠️ `the very community one aims to serve` → 「たくさんのコミュニティ」ではなく「まさにその（仕えようとしている）コミュニティ」。veryは強調の意味

**修正後の訳（参考）：**
> 災害下における効果的なリーダーシップは、リーダーが無敵さを示すことを求めるものではない。むしろ、自らの限界を認め、自分が仕えようとしているまさにそのコミュニティから支援を求める勇気を要求する。

- 総評：**B**

---

## Step 2: English Response（英語コメント）

**健さんのコメント：**
> This is a valid view, however, the leader who lead the project invulnerability, the decision making allows more quickly than the others. In the context of disaster, this aspects also has to consider unless it become dictatorship.

**修正案：**
> This is a valid view; however, a leader who projects invulnerability can often make decisions more quickly than those who openly acknowledge their limitations. In the context of disaster, this aspect must also be considered, to avoid such efficiency becoming a form of dictatorship.

**主な修正点：**

| 元の表現 | 修正後 | 理由 |
|----------|--------|------|
| `the leader who lead the project invulnerability` | `a leader who projects invulnerability` | 動詞leadが誤用。projectsが正しい |
| `the decision making allows more quickly` | `can make decisions more quickly` | allowsの主語が不適切 |
| `than the others` | `than those who openly acknowledge their limitations` | the othersが曖昧 |
| `this aspects also has to consider` | `this aspect must also be considered` | 単複一致・受動態 |
| `unless it become dictatorship` | `to avoid it becoming a form of dictatorship` | 接続詞の使い方が不自然 |

- 総評：**B**

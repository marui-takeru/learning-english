# Day 5 — 2026-06-05

## 📝 English Sentence

> *"Community-based participatory research challenges the traditional boundary between researcher and researched by positioning community members as active co-investigators, thereby ensuring that the research process itself becomes a vehicle for empowerment and locally grounded knowledge production."*

**Topic:** Community-Based Participatory Research (CBPR)

---

## Step 1: Translation（日本語訳）

**健さんの訳：**
> コミュニティ参加型研究は、伝統的な境界であった研究者と研究対象として位置付けられるコミュニティメンバーが共同で発見作業を行い、そのプロセスを通じて、研究者が地域住民のエンパワメントにつながり、地域の知見を発見する取り組みへ昇華するチャレンジである。

**フィードバック：**
- ⚠️ `challenges the traditional boundary` → 「挑戦する・問い直す」という動詞の意味が訳に出ていない
- ⚠️ `co-investigators` → 「発見作業」は意訳過剰。「共同研究者」が正確
- ⚠️ `a vehicle for empowerment` → 「エンパワメントの手段となる」が正確
- ⚠️ `locally grounded knowledge production` → 「地域に根ざした知識の生産」が正確

**修正後の訳（参考）：**
> コミュニティ参加型研究は、コミュニティメンバーを能動的な共同研究者として位置づけることで、研究者と研究対象という伝統的な境界を問い直す。それによって研究プロセス自体が、エンパワメントと地域に根ざした知識生産の手段となる。

- 総評：**B+**

## Step 2: English Response（英語コメント）

**健さんのコメント：**
> This is valid view and recently I found this view. This view are proved by some researcher. If so, it come to a simple question, why only researcher can enhance to local residents through these research methods? This insights that the academic researchers have mysterious power.

**修正案：**
> This is a valid view, and I have recently come across this perspective in my own research. It has been supported by several researchers in the field. However, this raises a simple but fundamental question: why are only academic researchers assumed to have the capacity to empower local residents through these methods? This implies that researchers may possess a form of epistemic authority that itself reproduces the very hierarchy CBPR seeks to dismantle.

**主な修正点：**

| 元の表現 | 修正後 | 理由 |
|----------|--------|------|
| `This is valid view` | `This is a valid view` | 冠詞 `a` が必要 |
| `recently I found this view` | `I have recently come across this perspective` | 現在完了形・学術的表現 |
| `This view are proved by` | `This view has been supported by` | 単複一致・provedよりsupportedが学術的 |
| `it come to a simple question` | `this raises a simple but fundamental question` | 主語を明確に・動詞の活用 |
| `why only researcher can enhance to local residents` | `why only researchers are assumed to empower local residents` | enhanceの用法が不正確・empowerが適切 |
| `This insights that` | `This implies that` | insightは名詞。動詞はimply / suggest |
| `mysterious power` | `a form of epistemic authority` | 学術的表現への言い換え |

- 総評：**B+**
